And at just a dollar or two a pop, it's one of the cheapest meals I know.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of Ramen
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Scallion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 Side of Kimchi

Instructions

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water
  2. Put disc of noodles in water once boiling
  3. Put in other packaged ingredients of ramen
  4. After 2.5 minutes, crack egg on top
  5. Cook for 30 more seconds
  6. Garnish with scallions
  7. Serve with side of kimchi
